Welcome

Ye Olde Bookshop is an independently owned bookshop in Christchurch. We specialise in used books and are well known in New Zealand and overseas for our large selection of rare and first edition books. We are also experts in book restorations. If there is a book you are looking for, or you need a book lovingly restored, please get in touch.
