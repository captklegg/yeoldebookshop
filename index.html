Welcome

Ye Olde Bookshop is an independently owned bookshop in Christchurch. We specialise in used books and are well known in New Zealand and overseas for our large selection of rare and first edition books. We are also experts in book restorations. If you are searching for a book, would like a book valued, or you need a book lovingly restored, please get in touch.

Latest News

Author Reading

Sherrie McNib will visit Ye Olde Bookshop to read her new romance novel "The Lagoon". The story tells of two strangers marooned on a deserted Pacific island and their struggle to survive, and to discover... each other. There will also be a book signing, and tea and scones. October 27th, 7:30 pm

Book Promotion

Local author and friend of Ye Olde Bookshop, Digby Clifton, will be in store to promote his new book "The Soup Diaries", an Agatha Christie-style murder/mystery set in the kitchen of a high society restaurant. Tea and soup will be served. November 7th, 6:00 pm

New Release

In store now, the new romance novel "Casino Girl" by Christchurch author Andy Simons is available. Can a couple find each other ten years after a romantic one-off meeting at a roulette table? This is a print-on-demand book and available in limited numbers. Get yours quick!
