Book of the Month

This month's book is "The Old Man and the Sea" by the American author, Ernest Hemingway.

Set in the Gulf Stream off the coast of Havana, Hemingway's magnificent fable is the tale of an old man, a young boy and a giant fish.

We have a first edition copy of this wonderful book in stock. It is in VG condition, and has the letter "A" printed on the copyright page, as well as the Scribner seal, meaning it is a genuine first edition book.

About the Author

Ernest Miller Hemingway was born on July 21, in Cicero, Illinois, and died on July 2, 1961.

He was a novelist and short-story writer, and was awarded the Nobel Prize for Literature in 1954.

His writing was noted for its masculinity, and Ernest himself was known for his adventurous and widely publicised life.

His writing style had a strong influence on American and British fiction in the 20th century.
